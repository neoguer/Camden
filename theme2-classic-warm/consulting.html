College Consulting

Camden offers comprehensive college consulting services for students pursuing top universities and conservatories.

Having successfully navigated the admissions process at multiple Ivy League institutions and elite dual degree programs, Camden provides personalized guidance to help students achieve their academic and musical aspirations.

University Admissions

Strategic guidance for applications to top universities, including Ivy League schools, with expertise in essays, interviews, and positioning

Dual Degree Programs

Conservatory Applications

Application Strategy

Expertise & Background

Camden was admitted to numerous Ivy League universities and holds a B.A. from Harvard University. He has successfully gained admission to most of the nation's top dual degree music programs, combining elite academic institutions with premier conservatories. He currently studies at The Juilliard School, giving him firsthand experience with both traditional university and conservatory pathways.
