Teaching

Camden is currently accepting new cello students of all levels.

As an assistant conductor and chamber music coach at Boston Youth Symphony Orchestras, Camden brings a wealth of experience in musical education. His teaching philosophy emphasizes technical excellence, musical expression, and creative programming.

Private Lessons

One-on-one instruction tailored to each student's goals and abilities, from beginners to advanced players

Chamber Music Coaching

Ensemble coaching drawing from experience with world-class chamber musicians

Performance Preparation

Audition and recital preparation with insights from performances at Carnegie Hall and beyond

Theory & Musicianship

Comprehensive musical education incorporating history, theory, and interpretation

Educational Background

Camden studied with distinguished pedagogues including Joel Krosnick, Paul Katz, Sieun Lin, and Adam Grabois, and currently studies with David Finckel and Nina Lee at The Juilliard School. He holds a B.A. in History from Harvard University.
